Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Hallo World Publisher Lab may collect basic account information, authorization status, and access tokens after a user grants permission through a supported platform's official authorization flow.

2. How We Use Information

Information is used to connect user accounts, display account status, and publish content that the user has explicitly approved.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. We do not share user data with third parties except as required to operate the requested platform integrations or comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Retention and Revocation

Users may revoke access through the connected platform settings. When access is revoked, related tokens should no longer be used by the app.

5. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through the GitHub account that hosts this site.
